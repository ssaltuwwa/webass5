Frequently Asked Questions

What are your working hours? +

We are open Monday-Friday: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM, Saturday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM, and Sunday: 11:00 AM - 5:00 PM.

Do I need to make an appointment? +

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making an appointment to ensure availability and minimize waiting time.

What safety measures do you have? +

We follow strict hygiene protocols, including sterilizing all tools, using disposable items when possible, and maintaining a clean environment.

How long does a manicure last? +

A regular manicure typically lasts 1-2 weeks, while gel manicures can last 2-3 weeks with proper care.
